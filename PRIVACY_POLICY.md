# Privacy Policy — ToolBox

**Effective date:** 2026-03-31

ToolBox (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a client-side, single-page web application. Our slogan is **“Your online Swiss Army Knife.”**

## Summary
- ToolBox runs entirely in your browser.
- We do not require accounts and we do not collect personal data.
- Some tools may contact third-party services only to perform the function you requested (for example, live currency conversion).
- Your preferences (such as favourites and theme) are stored locally on your device.

## Data We Collect
ToolBox does **not** collect, store, or sell personal information.

We do not run ads, and we do not use analytics trackers.

## Data Stored on Your Device (Local Storage)
ToolBox uses your browser’s `localStorage` to remember:
- Light/dark theme preference
- Favourite tools
- Optional tool settings (if the tool supports saving them)

This data stays on your device and can be removed at any time by clearing your browser’s site data/storage.

## Third-Party Services
Some tools may make requests to third-party APIs to provide up-to-date results. For example:
- **Exchange rates** may be fetched from **ExchangeRate-API** (`exchangerate-api.com`).

When you use these features:
- Your browser will send the request directly to the third-party service.
- ToolBox does not receive or store the data that the third-party service processes.
- The third-party service may log technical information (such as your IP address) as part of providing the service.

Please review the third-party provider’s privacy policy for more details.

## Your Inputs
Any text, numbers, files, or other content you enter into ToolBox is processed locally in your browser, except where a tool clearly requires an online request (such as currency conversion). ToolBox does not upload or store your inputs on a ToolBox server because ToolBox has no backend server.

## Children’s Privacy
ToolBox is not designed to collect personal data from anyone, including children. If you believe personal information has been submitted to a third-party service through ToolBox, please contact that service directly.

## Security
We take reasonable steps to keep the app safe, but no website or internet transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for the information you choose to enter into the app.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective date” at the top of this page.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository for ToolBox.
